## Introduction

IO设备的三个特性：

- **Bahavior**
	- Input(read once), output(write only, cannote read), or storage(can be reread and usually rewritten)
- **Partner**
	- Human, or machine
- **Data rate**
	- 与存储部件交换数据的最大速率

IO设备的性能指标：

- **Throughput**
- **Response Time**
